Tucher Group Introduction

Tucher Group is the holding company of the businesses of the Tucher Family, around Thomas von Tucher and his son Florian von Tucher. The Tucher are an old and traditional German family and were first mentioned in 1309. During 14th and 15th century the Tucher Family made its fortune by trading from the central city of Nuremberg to the rest of Europe, especially Venice, which was the European end of the Silk road at that time.

Nowadays, the Tucher Group is leading in the fields of project development, investment and engineering, with a clear focus on Europe and China as well as the countries along the New Silk Road under “One Belt, One Road”.

The Chairman of the Tucher Group is Mr. Florian von Tucher who is a civil engineer with extensive China experience. He started the China business for the Tucher Group in 2005 and managed it before being promoted to be the Chairman of Tucher Group in 2015.
