About 3i Development

The 3i GmbH is the developing arm of Tucher Group with a focus on infrastructure impact investments in emerging markets. With one of Germany’s biggest engineering groups behind it and a strong track record in the development of solar power projects worldwide, 3i GmbH possesses the required experience and network in the field for the successful realization.
